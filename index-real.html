Welcome to APPT 2021

Welcome to the official website of the 14th International Symposium on Adavanced Parallel Program Thechnoloy (APPT 2021). APPT 2021 is Technically Co-Sponsored by CCF. conference will be held in SP, outside China August 2021.

The conference will serve as an international forum for the presentation and exchange of technological advances and research results in the broad fields of distributed systems design, development and evaluation. The conference will bring together leading researchers, engineers and scientists in the domain of interest from around the world.

We warmly welcome prospective authors to submit your research papers to APPT 2021, and share your latest research results and valuable experiences with other top-scientists, engineers and scholars from all over the world.
