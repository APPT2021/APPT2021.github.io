Call for Papers


APPT is a flagship biennial international conference of the China Computer Federation Technical Committee on Computer Architecture (TCArch). It has become the major forum for Chinese computer architects and system designers to better engage with international colleagues, develop better mutual understanding, and build stronger academic ties. It aims to provide an ideal environment for researchers in both academia and industry to present thought-provoking ideas related to all aspects of computer architecture. The 2021 APPT conference is scheduled to be held ouside of China, in summer 2021.

We warmly welcome prospective authors to submit your research papers to APPT 2021, and share your latest research results and valuable experiences with other top-scientists, engineers and scholars from all over the world. The authors of the best-ranked papers of APPT will be invited to submit the Journal of Computer Science and Technology(JCST).

Topics of Interest

We invite original paper submissions related to (but not limited to) the following topics:

  • Accelerators
  • Parallel Software
  • Architecture applications of Machine Learning
  • Caches
  • Cloud, datacenter, cluster/distributed systems
  • Embedded, IoT
  • Emerging technologies
  • FPGAs and Reconfigurable
  • GPUs

Paper Submission


Submissions should follow the guidelines and formatting rules specified on the conference website. Papers that violate these guidelines and rules may be returned to author(s) without review.

You are invited to submit papers in the regular paper form, with 12-point font, in a two-column format, including figures, and tables. Submitted papers must use the official SIGPLAN Latex or MS Word templates from http://jcst.ict.ac.cn/EN/column/column109.shtml. Please note that you should not modify key aspects of the template, such as font face, spacing, etc.

Papers will be reviewed and selected by the program committee consisting of experts in the area. Emphasis will be on original work that exposes a new problem, advocates a specific solution, or reports on actual experience. We welcome submissions of rejected papers from the conference. Accepted papers will be recommended and published in the Journal of Computer Science and Technology. One author of each accepted paper is required to attend the workshop and present the paper for it to be included in the proceedings.

We will use XXX to manage submissions and reviews. Paper submission is double-blind. Submissions should be made online at here. If you have any question regarding paper submission, please do not hesitate to contact the conference PC Chairs: Dr. Chao Li
